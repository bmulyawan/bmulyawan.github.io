E2 Home Tutoring

Because we believe Learning is Contagious!

E2 provides individualized learning programs for students. Every student is assessed and interviewed to determine what type of learning is going to provide the most positive results.

E2 provides a balance between tutoring and mentoring to help students and families attain goals set forward. Each student has different expectations and goals. Through individualized and differentiated programs, E2 guides students on a learning process that allows them to succeed one goal at a time.

E2 has dedicated time and energy to helping students become life long learners. Area of services: Ropes Crossing, St Marys, Penrith areas.
