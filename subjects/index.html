Primary School

Primary school forms the foundational understanding that will ultimately determine a large part of the success your son or daughter will achieve at High School. We offer Primary School Tuition in:

  • Mathematics and Numeracy Skills (K-6)

High School

The marks that your son or daughter achieve during their secondary schooling years will determine what direction their tertiary education travels in, and ultimately what lays ahead for the rest of their adult life. We offer tutors in all Secondary School subjects, including:

  • English (all levels years 7-12)
  • Maths (all levels years 7-12)
